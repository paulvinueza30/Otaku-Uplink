Key partners

Who mutually benefits from our buisness?

Musuvi Inc

Since Japanese product companies rarely export their goods to other countries.We have partnered up with Musuvi Inc. They help individuals who love Japanese goods by importing Japanese-made products to countries such as the United States. Luckily for us we have partnered with them to help us import Japanese goods such as Manga, Sake, Ramune, etc.

alienware

Alienware has sponsored us to help us deliver our clients the fastest computers for gaming and browsing the web. 20 high speed computers all with a nice bulk discount, thanks Alienware!

Our local community

Otaku Uplink is meant to be a safe space to come and relax, what better place is there to host school events/fundraisers? On top of that we would also love to run charity events to support a specific cause, any profits we make 75% will be donated back to the charity. Lastly, as the prime-gaming spot we would love to be a venue for gaming competitions with the prize pool coming from the host of the competition.

Redbull

You can't have a gamer lounge without gamer fuel. In our lounge you will find numerous Redbull fridges to keep you on your grind for hours on end. They will be Otaku Uplink's energy drink.
