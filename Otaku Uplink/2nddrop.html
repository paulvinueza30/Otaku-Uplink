Revenue Streams and Cost Structure

All about the money

Source of the money?

Our business generates profits through the purchase of food and beverages, as well as purchases towards the usage of our high tech computers, and gaming lounge.

What are you paying for?

On top of the value of the various imported snacks and beverages from Japan, our customers will also be paying for the experience of enjoying an authentic Japanese atmosphere.

Cost Structure

  • Kitchen license: $3000-5000
  • Kitchen Cost: $13,000
  • Internet: $4200, $375/month
  • Liquor licence: $1820
  • Employee Wages: (Estimated 45k per year per employee)
  • Food and Beverage Costs: (Estimated 4k cost per month) Noodle cup, 86 Soda can, 25 Mochi cakes, 15 to-go Sushi.
  • Manga: (comics purchased from Japan upon release) ($20 per copy)
  • Machines: 20x Gaming Desktops $20,000 , 10 consoles (5 playstation and 5 Xbox) $5,000
  • Rent: $26,250 a year for 850 sq ft space.
  • Total: $124,270
