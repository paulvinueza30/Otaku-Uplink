Key Activities

Production Operations

A main source of profit generated by the business will be through the various items available on our menu

Computer and Equipment Rentals: Another source of profit our business will focus on will be the computers available for short term rentals. These rentals will also be available for large groups as well and can be used for large scale video-game tournaments or other activities.

Problem-solving Administration

Most of the problem solving necessary can be handled through the employees hired. Our employees will be well trained to handle the various scenarios in which they find themselves in. This training in considered essential within our business, as the experience of our customers is the forefront of our priority.

It is understandable for a situation to occur in which our employees may be unable to produce a solution. It is for this reason that internal management positions exist, and play a crucial role within the business. Managers are expected to be leads within the business, providing help to customers as well as their staff.
